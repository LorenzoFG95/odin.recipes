Grandma's secret cookies

Description

Awww you poor sweet baby... You've been working all day, havent'you? Don't you worry my dear, grandma baked her delicious chocolate chip cookies. They are still warm and soft. DIP them in your coffee!

This recipe is great for an afternoon snack. It makes a tray of a dozen cookies. Don't forget to wait a little for them to cool off before eating them!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all-pupose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • secret ingredient n 2
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • secret ingredient n 3

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F
  2. Mix flour and baking soda in a bowl
  3. Cream togheter butter and sugars
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  5. Add the secret ingredient n 2
  6. Add the chocolate chips and mix well
  7. Roll the dough into balls and place them on a tray
  8. Sprinkle secret ingredient n 3 on top of the cookies
  9. Bake in preheated oven for 8/10 minutes
  10. Let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack
  11. Enjoy!
